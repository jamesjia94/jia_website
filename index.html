About Me

I am a Master's student with a concentration in Data Science and Systems at University of California, Berkeley.

Currently, I am working on porting BIDData onto Apache Spark.

For the past two summers, I worked at Apple as a software engineering intern where I designed a screen capturing module for multi-machine automation and a cross-functional reporting system for automated test results.

Although most of my corporate experience has been developing tools and accompanying web development, I have experience ranging from MIPS and Intel x86 assembly programming, to building a reliable transport protocol on top of UDP datagrams, to writing a pacman agent fueled solely by heursitics and Markov Trees.

Learn Algebra

Algebra tutoring application for the Android (iOS version in development) that features an abridged algebra textbook, problem generators and explanations, a graphing interface, and statistics tracking.

RSS Feed Reader

An iOS application that allows users to track their favorite RSS Feeds. It also detects when Internet is no longer available and pushes a notification to alert the user regarding the network change.

GeoNotification App

Simple application that allows users to receive a notification whenever they walk within a certain radius of a particular location. It can be customized to only notify at a certain time as well.

Udora

A simple Web app that is similar in concept to Pandora, but for music videos. When users input a particular YouTube video, Udora will stream a recommended playlist of similar videos.
